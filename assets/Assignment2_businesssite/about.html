Who we are


IPPSec Inc is a public safety company that was born after 14 years of experiential research while performing offensive and defensive Information, Personnel, and Physical Security operations all over the world. From that, and more than 8 years of need validation from security specialists in 22 countries, it was determined that there is a palpable need for a new safety and security solution that is built completely from the ground up using new, novel, and future thinking concepts.

The goals of IPPSec is to create a new generation security platform that uses distributed sensing and computing methods coupled with proprietary security improvement techniques, that will make the platform fit for a smart city of the future. The platform will be created to deliver tangible savings, security improvements, current system optimizations, community cohesion strengthening, and real-time organizational metrics.

Justin Pelletier, PhD

Advisor on IP Generation and Testing

Dr. Justin Pelletier is the national director of the Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition and the business director for Security Assessment and Forensic Examination (SAFE) Lab in Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) Center for Cybersecurity . He holds a PhD in Information Assurance and Security, an MBA in Entrepreneurship and a BS in Computer Science. Dr. Pelletier is an officer in the Army Reserve and holds a Top-Secret Clearance. He is a former member of the National Security Council’s Information Systems Working Group for modeling and simulation. His current research interests include the economics of information security, intelligent deception detection, and ethics in intelligent combat systems.
